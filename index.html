Our Comprehensive Services

Occupational Therapy

Enhancing daily living skills and independence.

  • Focus on sensory integration and motor skills
  • Adaptive equipment support
  • Fine motor skill training for children with autism

Stroke & Hand Rehabilitation

Restoring function with advanced therapies.

  • Constraint-Induced Movement Therapy (CIMT)
  • 60% of stroke patients regain significant hand function

Special Education

Personalized programs to support cognitive development.

  • Individualized Education Programs (IEPs)
  • Aligned with each child's learning style

Specialized Therapies for Every Need

ABA Therapy

Evidence-based autism intervention through positive reinforcement.

  • 85% success rate in social skills improvement.

Speech & Language Therapy

Improving communication, articulation, and social interaction.

  • 70% improvement in clarity within six months.

Psychotherapy & CBT

Identify developmental, emotional, or behavioral issues in children and adolescents.

Offer therapy for children, teens, and adults dealing with psychological or emotional challenges

  • Focus on anxiety and depression relief.

Innovative Therapies for Holistic Well-being

Sports and Group Therapy

Enhances social skills and physical health via team activities.

  • Inclusive programs for individuals with disabilities.
